Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 3, 2025  ·  Last Updated: May 3, 2025

Zenna Mind ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use the Zenna Mind mobile application.
01

Who This Policy Applies To

Zenna Mind is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

02

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the app experience. Below is a summary of the data we store:

Data When Collected Type
Anonymous user ID (UID) On first app launch Auto
Email address Only if you create an account (not available in current version) Optional
Display name Only if you create an account (not available in current version) Optional
Profile photo URL Only if you sign in with Google or Apple (not available in current version) Optional
Meditation session history When you complete a session Usage
Streak & statistics When you complete a session Usage
Favorite sessions When you bookmark a session (not available in current version) Usage
App settings & preferences When you change settings Usage
Last active timestamp Each time you open the app Auto

We do not collect location data, contacts, camera access, or any data unrelated to your meditation practice.

03

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize your meditation experience
  • To track your streak and progress over time
  • To save your favorite sessions and preferences (favorites not available in current version)
  • To send optional meditation reminders (only if you enable notifications)
  • To maintain your account if you choose to create one (not available in current version)

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

04

Anonymous Usage

You can use Zenna Mind without creating an account. When you first open the app, an anonymous session is created automatically using Firebase Authentication. No personal information is required.

Account creation (email, Google, Apple sign-in) is not available in the current version and may be introduced in a future update. If implemented, your existing anonymous data (history, streak) will be preserved and linked to your new account.

05

Third-Party Services

Zenna Mind uses the following Google Firebase services to operate:

  • Firebase Authentication — manages anonymous user sessions. Google, Apple, and email sign-in are not available in the current version
  • Cloud Firestore — stores your profile, history, streak, and preferences
  • Firebase Storage — hosts audio files and session images

Firebase is operated by Google LLC. Data may be stored on Google's servers. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not use any other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

06

Sign-In with Apple & Google

Sign-in with Apple or Google is not available in the current version of the app. If introduced in a future update, we would receive basic profile information (name, email, profile photo) from those providers solely to create and identify your account. We would not store your Apple or Google password.

07

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your app data is active. If you delete your app data, your personal data (history, streak, preferences) is permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days.

Session history records are kept in an anonymized form to preserve the integrity of completed entries. Audio and thumbnail files hosted on Firebase Storage are not personal data and are retained independently.

08

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account

You can delete your account directly within the app, or by contacting us at the email below.

09

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Access to user data in Firestore is restricted by Security Rules — users can only read and write their own data. Master content (sessions, categories, moods) is read-only for all users.

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to keep your device secure.

10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
